History

There are two histories, which are saved separate: The history of exactly one wine is stored within the wine database, and can be changed by editing the wine itself in the wine editor.
The second is a global history including all wines, and is also saved separate; It is not updated if you change the history in the wine editor - it's like a written book.

Both histories are splitted in buying and drinking.

Wine History

Can be found inside of the wine editor. Use the »In« and »Out« button to update it. An already done entry can be edited by simply clicking on it ...

The wine history is stored inside of the wines database file, means after explicit saving your modifications. But: A second entry goes directly to the global history and is saved immediately.

Global History

NOTE: Changes from the »In« and »Out« button affect the global history without saving; So if you exit the wine editor without saving, your changes to the global history are already saved anyway.

To view it have a look at the main windows menubar - it opens a separate window, which shows you any bought or drunken wine and some interesting summaries. Any entry you made to the wine history will also be added to the global history - but as the global history is stored separate, later changes of the wine history entries won't affect the global history - in fact it's like a written book. A timeline of the wine you bought and drunk.

Note that therefore you should first fill out the complete dataset of a wine, and enter your purchase or empty bottle later. Otherwise a missing price (for example) won't affect this history anymore until you change it in there manually.
